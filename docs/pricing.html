Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay to use Lumen AI?

No. The Basic plan is completely free and gives you unlimited access to local AI models via Ollama. You only need a paid plan if you want access to cloud models like GPT-4o or Claude.

What happens when I hit my token limit?

Cloud models will be paused until your next billing period. You can purchase a token top-up to continue immediately. Local Ollama models are always available regardless of token usage.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

Do top-ups change my model access?

No. Top-ups only add tokens. A Pro subscriber who buys top-ups still only has access to GPT-4o Mini. Upgrade to Max for all cloud models.
