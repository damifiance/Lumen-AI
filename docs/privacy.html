Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

1. Overview

Lumen AI is designed with privacy in mind. Your PDFs and documents are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

If you create an account (required for paid plans), we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile information you choose to provide (bio, institution, research interests)
  • Authentication tokens

Account data is stored in Supabase (hosted on AWS).

2.2 Subscription and Payment Data

Payment processing is handled by our partners:

  • Paddle (international users): Processes payments as Merchant of Record. We receive your subscription status and customer ID — not your payment card details.
  • PortOne (Korean users): Processes payments through Korean PGs. We receive transaction confirmations — not your card details.

We store: subscription tier, token usage counts, billing period dates, and provider customer IDs.

2.3 AI Chat Data

When you use cloud AI models (Pro/Max plans), the text you send (selected passages, questions) is transmitted to third-party AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic) for processing. These providers have their own privacy policies. We record token usage counts (not the content of your messages).

When you use local AI (Ollama), all processing happens on your device. No data is sent to any external server.

2.4 Documents and Files

Your PDFs, highlights, and annotations are stored entirely on your local device. They are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party service.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the service (authentication, subscription management)
  • To enforce token limits and subscription tiers
  • To process payments through our payment partners
  • To communicate important service updates

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as needed to provide the service (authentication, payments, cloud AI processing).

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Account and subscription data: Supabase (PostgreSQL on AWS)
  • Authentication: Supabase Auth with encrypted tokens
  • Payment data: Handled by Paddle/PortOne (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Local data: Stored in your device's filesystem and local SQLite database

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in your profile
  • Correct inaccurate profile information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your profile data

To exercise these rights, use the account settings in the app or contact us via GitHub Issues.

6. Cookies and Tracking

The desktop application does not use cookies or tracking pixels. The website (this site) does not use analytics or tracking cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

Lumen AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us via GitHub Issues.
